========================================
FIRE NOC TERMS & CONDITIONS
========================================

Fire and Emergency Services Department

By obtaining a Fire No Objection Certificate (Fire NOC), the applicant agrees to comply with the following conditions:

1. The premises shall maintain all fire safety systems in working condition at all times.

2. Fire extinguishers, hydrants, alarms, smoke detectors, and sprinkler systems shall be regularly inspected and maintained.

3. Emergency exits and escape routes shall remain unobstructed and clearly marked.

4. Fire safety signage shall be installed as per applicable regulations.

5. Electrical installations shall comply with prescribed safety standards.

6. The premises shall not store flammable, explosive, or hazardous materials beyond approved limits.

7. Firefighting equipment shall be easily accessible and available for immediate use.

8. Employees shall receive basic fire safety and emergency evacuation training.

9. Fire drills shall be conducted periodically as required by the competent authority.

10. Any structural modifications affecting fire safety shall be reported to the Fire Department.

11. Building occupancy shall not exceed the approved capacity.

12. Emergency lighting systems shall remain functional during power failures.

13. Fire pumps, water storage tanks, and hydrant systems shall be operational at all times.

14. The applicant shall permit inspections by authorized Fire Department officials.

15. Any deficiency identified during inspections shall be rectified within the prescribed period.

16. The Fire NOC does not exempt the applicant from complying with any other statutory requirements.

17. Renewal applications must be submitted before the certificate expiry date.

18. Failure to maintain fire safety standards may result in suspension or cancellation of the Fire NOC.

19. Serious violations may lead to closure of the premises until compliance is restored.

20. The applicant is responsible for ensuring public safety within the premises.

========================================
HIGH-RISK VIOLATIONS
========================================

The following may result in immediate action:

- Blocked emergency exits
- Non-functional fire alarms
- Absence of fire extinguishers
- Unsafe electrical systems
- Unauthorized storage of hazardous materials
- Overcrowding beyond approved limits

========================================
PENALTIES
========================================

Non-compliance may result in:

- Monetary penalties
- Suspension of Fire NOC
- Closure orders
- Legal proceedings
- Cancellation of occupancy permissions

========================================
ACKNOWLEDGEMENT
========================================

By applying for or renewing a Fire NOC, the applicant confirms that all fire safety measures are maintained and agrees to comply with applicable fire safety regulations and departmental guidelines.
