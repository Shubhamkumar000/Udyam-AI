========================================
SHOP & ESTABLISHMENT REGISTRATION
TERMS & CONDITIONS
========================================

Labour Department

By obtaining Shop & Establishment Registration, the employer agrees to comply with the following conditions:

1. The establishment shall operate only from the registered address.

2. The registration certificate shall be displayed prominently at the premises.

3. Accurate records of employees shall be maintained.

4. Working hours shall comply with applicable labour laws.

5. Employees shall receive wages as prescribed under applicable laws.

6. Weekly holidays and leave benefits shall be provided as required.

7. Child labour is strictly prohibited.

8. The establishment shall maintain a safe and healthy working environment.

9. Female employees shall be provided protections as mandated by law.

10. Any change in ownership, address, business activity, or employee strength shall be reported to the Labour Department.

11. Registers and records shall be produced during inspections.

12. The establishment shall comply with minimum wage requirements.

13. Employee attendance records shall be maintained.

14. The employer shall comply with all applicable labour welfare provisions.

15. The establishment shall not engage in unlawful business activities.

16. Registration shall be renewed before expiry where applicable.

17. Authorities may inspect the establishment without prior notice.

18. Violations may attract penalties under applicable labour laws.

19. False information provided during registration may result in cancellation.

20. Continued operation without valid registration may invite legal action.

========================================
EMPLOYER RESPONSIBILITIES
========================================

- Maintain employee records
- Ensure workplace safety
- Comply with labour regulations
- Display registration certificate
- Cooperate with inspections

========================================
PENALTIES
========================================

Failure to comply may result in:

- Fines
- Prosecution
- Registration suspension
- Registration cancellation

========================================
ACKNOWLEDGEMENT
========================================

By applying for or renewing Shop & Establishment Registration, the applicant confirms compliance with applicable labour and employment laws.
